French Toast

Description

It's hard to go wrong with a classic breakfast food like french toast, but, when everything is done just right, it can feel like a rare experience that you never want to end. Imagine you ordered your favorite french toast option from your go-to breakfast restaurant. Can you remember how good it tasted? This recipe is sure to rival that experience, if not be significantly better.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup milk
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 12 thick slices of bread
