French Toast

Description

It's hard to go wrong with a classic breakfast food like french toast, but, when everything is done just right, it can feel like a rare experience that you never want to end. Imagine you ordered your favorite french toast option from your go-to breakfast restaurant. Can you remember how good it tasted? This recipe is sure to rival that experience, if not be significantly better.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup milk
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 12 thick slices of bread

Steps

  1. In a large mixing bowl, add the flour. Whisk in milk slowly. Continue whisking as adding in eggs, sugar, vanilla extract, cinnamon, and salt until smooth.
  2. Bring a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan up to medium heat.
  3. Saturate bread slices in milk mixture.
  4. Cook bread in batches on preheated pan or griddle until golden brown on both sides. Serve hot.
