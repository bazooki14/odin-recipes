Lasagna

Description

The above image is an example of what the dish might look like. So long as the result at least slightly resembles what's above, the dish will undoubtably be worth the time it took to make. Feel free to not scale serving sizes based on the serving seen above.

Ingredients

  • 12 whole wheat lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 2 cloves garlic, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano (optional)
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • salt and ground black pepper (amount based on personal preference)
  • 16 ounces cottage cheese
  • 1/2 cup shredded Parmesan cheese
  • 2 eggs
  • 4 1/2 cups tomato-basil pasta sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degreed F
  2. Boil large pot of lightly salted water. Add in lasagna noodles and cook for 10 minutes or until al dente. Drain water when finished.
  3. In the meantime, into a large skillet over medium heat, add ground beef, garlic, oregano, garlic powder, salt and black pepper. Stir and cook until beef is browned and crumbly (approx. 10 min.).
  4. In a large bowl mix eggs, cottage cheese, and parmesan cheese until thoroughly combined.
  5. On the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking pan, lay 4 noodles side by side. Atop the noodles put a layer of prepared tomato-basil sauce, a layer of ground beef mixture, and a layer of cottage cheese mixture. Repeat layers two more times and finsh with a layer of sauce and mozzarella cheese. Cover the dish with aluminum foil.
  6. Bake until lasagna is bubbling and the cheese has melted (approx. 30 min.). Remove foil and continue baking until cheese begins to brown (approx. 10 min.). Remove from oven and let cool for at least 10 minutes before serving.
