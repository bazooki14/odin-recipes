Meatloaf

Description

A classic meatloaf recipe glazed with a homemade sauce. Your mom already asked for the recipe to this one so don't just take my word for it, this is a great, simple dish for anyone.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup ketchup
  • 1/3 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup lemon juice, divided
  • 1 teaspoon mustard powder
  • 2 pounds ground beef
  • 3 slices bread, broken up into small pieces
  • 1/4 cup chopped onion
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1 teaspoon beef bouillon granules

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F
  2. In a small bowl, combine 1 tablespoon lemon juice, mustard powder, brown sugar, and ketchup.
  3. In a separate larger bowl, combine remaining lemon juice, egg, onion, ground beef, bread, bouillon, and 1/3 of the ketchup mixture made in the previous step. Place in a 5x9 inch loaf pan.
  4. Bake for 1 hour, drain excess fat, coat with rest of ketchup mixture, and bake for 10 more minutes.
